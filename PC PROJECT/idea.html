So folks, keeping in mind all the problems stated in our site, we would like to introduce an idea through which this crime of cyber bullying can be controlled up to a certain limit.
We would like to present our idea of an official identity card that should be issued by the government to all the citizens of our country.

Digital Identity Card

Digital identity is essentially any personal data existing online that can be traced back to the real you. A digital identity may also be referred to as a digital subject or digital entity and is the digital representation of a set of claims made by one party about itself or another person, group, thing or concept. A Digital identity card is a small document issues by government as a proof that the information being displayed by the individual is orignal.

This is an identity card who’s number should be demanded by all the social media sites, if the individual wants to access their services The card we are advising to make is an identity card, which should be made compulsory to be linked to all the social accounts of the individual. So we can track that whether the person we are talking to is real or is he pretending to be someone else. This would lead to minimal generation of fake accounts. The name mentioned on the digital identity card should be used by all the social media platforms to use it as the user name. This would prevent a lot of fake accounts. This could also help authorities can identify/track the culprit very easily Through this we could restrict and investigate the account(s) of a person

The age barrier could restrict the content shown to the minor, on all the platforms with the help of this card This card can be used to restrict the minimum age-limit to use any of the social media platform, for example the minimum age to use facebook is 16yrs but many minor access by using false data and use this platform to get addicted, bullied and hence become a victim of cyber bullying. The card, when linked to youtube, can be used to prevent all the addictive and bad-stuff from being seeing before the sensible mature age.

Think before you post dialog box

We have to stop the cyberbullying at the source before the damage was done. Cyberbullying is a huge problem and there are no effective solutions to stop cyberbullying and that needed to change but more interestingly most of the teenagers are involved in the cyberbullying. This is because Adolescent brain is like a car with no brakes , no stopping, no thinking, no pausing just acting. They make impulsive, rash decisions which leads to cyberbullying . What if the reason these teenagers write offensive messages because they couldn't make good decisions on social media. What if we give them a chance to do that. What if we warn them before they're about to write something offensive.There is this saying - Think before you Speak! But in our world of technology and social media where our friends, family, loved ones are connected to each other through social media we need to Amend this old saying to
- THINK BEFORE YOU POST. By this if anyone tried to post an offensive message on social media then a Rethink dialog box will appear on their screen asking them to Hold on ! They are about to send an offensive message to someone that can really hurt them. Are you sure to send this message? Would they still be as willing to do it?
The answer is NO.
According to a study 93 % times when anyone receives an alert that says the above mentioned things, they changed their minds. This sudden change could be the realization from inner conscience or maybe fear But with it we are able to decrease the willingness to post an offensive message.
RETHINK BEFORE THE DAMAGE IS DONE IS AN EFFECTIVE LONG TERM SOLUTION TO STOP CYBERBULLYING.
